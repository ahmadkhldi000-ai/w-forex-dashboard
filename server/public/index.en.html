About the project

A professional, fully-automated gold trading system

W Forex combines a proven smart-grid strategy with strict capital protection. It runs 24/5 on MetaTrader 5, places limit orders along a dynamic grid, trails winners, and never risks more than the drawdown you set — then reports every action to this dashboard and to your Telegram channel in real time.

🧠

Trend-aware grid

A 200-period moving average filters direction so the bot only builds positions in line with the dominant trend — buy levels in uptrends, sell levels in downtrends.

📐

Dynamic risk per trade

Lot size is calculated from your balance and a configurable risk percentage, with ATR-based stop-losses that adapt to current market volatility.

🛡️

Hard drawdown cap

If floating + closed losses exceed your maximum drawdown, the bot instantly closes everything, cancels pending orders and stops — protecting your capital.

📏

Spread protection

Trading is automatically blocked when the spread spikes beyond your limit, so you never enter a bad fill during news or thin liquidity.

🚀

Trailing profit

Winners are locked in with an intelligent trailing stop that follows price, capturing trends while protecting gains the moment momentum fades.

📲

Full transparency

Every open, close, profit and loss is broadcast live to this dashboard and pushed to a private Telegram channel — nothing happens in the dark.

How it works

From price tick to closed trade

1

Market is read

On every tick the bot reads price, ATR (volatility) and the 200 MA (trend) directly from your MT5 terminal.

2

Grid is built

A ladder of buy-limit and sell-limit levels is placed around price, spaced by your configured step, only in the trend direction.

3

Orders fill & manage

As price reaches a level, a position opens with a calculated lot and ATR stop. Profits are taken at target; winners are trailed.

4

Reported live

Each event streams instantly to this dashboard chart and is sent to Telegram — a complete, auditable record of every trade.
